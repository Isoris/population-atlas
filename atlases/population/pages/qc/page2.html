What this page shows
scaffold
Per-sample QC dashboard. Three sections:
  1. Coverage distribution — histogram of per-sample mean WGS coverage; outliers flagged
  2. Callable fraction — what fraction of the genome each sample contributes to the joint callable set
  3. Missingness — per-sample missing-genotype rate at the joint SNP set
  4. Contamination — VerifyBamID estimates (FREEMIX / CHIPMIX)
  5. Kinship-pruning status — kept / removed for the 81-sample unrelated subset
