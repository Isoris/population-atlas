Structure design — two rounds
scaffold · awaiting MODULE_2B
Hatchery cohorts violate Hardy-Weinberg by construction (siblings, full broodlines, related broodstock). Two-round structure analysis is required:
  1. Round 1 — all 226 samples → reflects family + broodline structure. Useful for visualizing the actual cohort composition; not a population-genetic K estimate.
  2. Round 2 — NAToRA-pruned 81 unrelated samples → cleaner K estimate for the population-level structure, free of within-family signal.
K clusters in this dataset reflect hatchery broodline structure, not species admixture. The cohort is pure C. gariepinus.
